How to make PIZZA?

LIST OF INGREDIENTS REQUIRED TO MAKE PIZZA

  • 1 cup all purpose flour
  • 50 mililitre tomato ketch up
  • 1/2 tomato
  • 1 onion
  • 1/2 teaspoon chilli flakes
  • 1/2 teaspoon sugar
  • 4 tablespoon processed cheese
  • 2 mushroom
  • 1/4 capsicum
  • 1/2 teaspoon oregmo
  • 1/4 cup mozzarella
  • 1/2 tablespoon dry yeast
  • water as required
STEPS TO MAKE PIZZA
  1. Prepare the pizza dough
  2. prepare the pizza base
    • Preheat the oven at 180 degree celcius
    • Put the pizza base in preheated oven
    • And bake it for 10 minutes
  3. Chop all the vegetables for the pizza
  4. Spread the sauce and veggies on the base
  5. Bake the pizza at 250 degree celsius for 10 minutes
  6. Once done,take out the baking tray and slice the pizza
  7. Sprinkle oregano and chiili flakes ass per your taste and serve HOT .
